How Scoring Works

When you complete a mini-game, your score is automatically transmitted to the LMS via secure postMessage communication and optional webhook integration.

Scores are formative assessments designed to provide immediate feedback on your understanding. Each game has specific passing criteria based on accuracy, completion time, and problem-solving approach.

Troubleshooting

  • Ensure pop-ups are allowed for this domain
  • Try the alternative direct link if embedded version fails
  • Refresh the page if score submission seems stuck
  • Contact support if problems persist

Privacy & Data Use

We are committed to protecting your privacy while providing effective learning analytics.

Data We Collect

When you play games and submit scores, we collect only the essential data needed for educational assessment:

  • {studentHandle, assignment, score, timeMs, metadata}
  • No passwords, personal information, or browsing data
  • No tracking cookies beyond essential session management

Data Usage & Retention

  • Who can see it: Your instructors and course administrators only
  • Retention period: Data is kept for the duration of the course plus one academic year
  • Third parties: Hosting via Netlify/Vercel, code repositories on GitHub
🔒 Privacy Policy 📜 Terms of Use
Third-Party Platform Disclaimer: We use third-party platforms (e.g., StackBlitz/CodeSandbox/GitHub) for coding practice. By playing or submitting work, you'll use their services and may need accounts. Please review their Terms and Privacy Policies.
